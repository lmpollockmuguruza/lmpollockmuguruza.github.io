I remain agnostic on what it will find, but if you pinch me I’d say it will not be able to create novel ideas, not necessarily because it is bad at doing so even with proper supervision, but because novel ideas are often papers someone decided not to try (or tried) for a variety of reasons (e.g., lack of quality data, inappropriate methods, null results), and an AI may overestimate it’s capacity to go beyond these (or simply lacks the private information of select researchers).
