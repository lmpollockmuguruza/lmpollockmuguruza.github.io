About

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Political Science and Political Economy at the London School of Economics, where I focus on the intersection of economic methods and political analysis.

My research interests center on political economy, with particular emphasis on corruption and inequality. I apply quantitative methods to understand how institutional factors shape economic and political outcomes across different contexts.

I hold dual nationality (Spanish and American) and work part-time as a Policy Analyst at Broadpeak Advisory AG, where I conduct research on international development, technology policy, and governance.
