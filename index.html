About

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Political Science and Political Economy at the London School of Economics and Political Science, with a predicted Distinction (74.6%), graduating in December 2025. My work focuses on causal inference, corruption, and inequality, with extensive training in quantitative political economy. I work primarily in R for data analysis and visualisation. Here you will find examples of my projects (assignments, reappraisals) and my curriculum vitae.
